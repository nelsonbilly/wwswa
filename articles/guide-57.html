Evacuate in 20 Minutes: Load Plan & Checklist

Category: Preparedness & Gear • Last updated 2025-08-10

Objective

Leave fast with everything that matters using a pre‑planned load sequence and clear triggers.

Triggers

  • T1: Pack car; T2: Leave; T3: Leave now (skip extras).

Load Plan (Example SUV)

  1. Rear left: water/food bin.
  2. Rear right: shelter/sleep.
  3. Seat backs: first‑aid, comms.
  4. Cabin floor: docs/valuables.
  5. Roof/last: tools/fuel.

Checklist

  • IDs, meds, cash, drives
  • Go‑bags per person
  • Pet kit, carriers

Real Example

Family executed the plan in 14 minutes during a wildfire T2 call; nothing important left behind.

Contingencies

  • No time → grab go‑bags + docs and roll.
  • Two vehicles → duplicate essentials in both.

After‑Action

Time your dry runs quarterly and adjust bin weights.
