Field Repairs: Tape, Cord, Needle, Heat

Category: Preparedness & Gear • Last updated 2025-08-10

Objective

Keep critical gear functional with minimal tools so the mission continues.

Rapid Fixes

  • Boot sole delam: Clean/dry; tape wrap heel‑to‑toe; add cord circumferential lash.
  • Pack strap tear: Whip stitch with heavy thread; reinforce with webbing.
  • Tent pole crack: Splint with a repair sleeve or cut can; tape tight.
  • Stove jet clog: Clean with needle; keep spares.

Heat Tricks

  • Warm tape/adhesives inside jacket before use.

Real Example

A pack shoulder strap saved by a whip stitch + duct‑tape sandwich finished a 20 km exit with no further damage.

Checklist

  • Duct tape, tenacious tape
  • Heavy needle + thread
  • Webbing scraps, zip ties

Contingencies

  • Wet fabric → dry with body heat first.

After‑Action

Replace temporary tape with sewn patches at home.
