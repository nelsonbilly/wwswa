Vehicle Emergency Kit for Any Season

Objective

Create a vehicle emergency kit that works year-round in any climate, with redundancy for key systems.

Core Components

  • Water and purification tablets
  • High-calorie rations
  • Thermal blankets and layered clothing
  • Jumper cables, tire repair kit, air pump
  • LED flashlight with spare batteries
  • First aid kit with trauma supplies
  • Basic tools and multi-tool
  • Signaling devices: flares, whistle, panel

Seasonal Adjustments

In winter, add a small shovel, traction aids, and chemical hand warmers. In summer, carry extra water, shade tarp, and electrolyte packets.
