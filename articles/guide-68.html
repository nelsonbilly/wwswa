Suburban Home Security During Evac & Return

Category: Urban Survival • Last updated 2025-08-11

Objective

Reduce risk of burglary or damage when evacuating, and re‑enter safely post‑incident.

Before Leaving

  • Lock all entries; add wedges on vulnerable doors.
  • Shut off gas and water if advised; unplug non‑essentials.
  • Timer lights or neighbor checks if power expected.

Perimeter Notes

  • Photograph rooms and serial numbers; hide a copy off‑site.

On Return

  • Look for forced entry signs; don’t enter if suspicious—call authorities.
  • Check utilities deliberately; sniff for gas, look for water leaks and live wires.

Real Example

A family documented the house before a wildfire evac; when they returned, photos accelerated insurance and repairs.

Checklist

  • Door/window locks/wedges
  • Camera/phone for documentation
  • Gloves, flashlight for re‑entry

Contingencies

  • Power out → avoid fridges/freezers until prepared to dispose of contents.

After‑Action

Upgrade weak points (strikes, locks) and stage a printed evac checklist near the door.
