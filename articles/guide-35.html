Power Outage Security Measures

Category: Urban Survival • Last updated 2025-08-10

Objective

Maintain a safe home during outages with layered security and neighbor coordination.

Perimeter

  • Solar or battery motion lights; glow sticks to mark hazards.
  • Lock secondary entries (garage, side gates) and keep windows latched.

Interior

  • Lantern per room; keep one light on as a “power return” indicator.
  • Door wedges at night; bedroom key at hand; charged phone nearby.

Neighborhood

  • Buddy checks with neighbors; watch rotations if outage extends.

Real Example

A cul‑de‑sac set window schedules (lights out by 22:00) and a group text check every 3 hours; suspicious activity was noticed immediately.

Checklist

  • Extra locks/wedges
  • Motion lights
  • Charged power bank

Contingencies

  • Generator use → chain it; operate outside; lock gates.
  • Break‑in attempt → retreat to safe room and call authorities; avoid confrontation.

After‑Action

Add secondary locks and solar lights to permanent setup.
